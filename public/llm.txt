# GenAI Community EU

> Where Europe's GenAI builders actually meet — the European community of generative AI builders and practitioners, with local chapters across Europe, an expert blog, a weekly newsletter, and one annual summit in Valencia.

GenAI Community EU connects practitioners working with generative AI across Europe through local chapters (city meetups and hands-on workshops), an expert-written blog, a weekly newsletter, and one annual in-person gathering — GenAI Summit EU in Valencia, Spain. The focus is real production work: agentic architectures, evaluations, infrastructure, and deployment, not hype. The site is operated by My Tech Plan, S.L. The blog is published on Beehiiv (https://genaicommunity.beehiiv.com/).

## Pages

- [Home](https://genaicommunity.eu/): Overview of the community — what it is, the local chapters, the blog, and the annual GenAI Summit EU.
- [Chapters](https://genaicommunity.eu/chapters): Local groups across European cities (e.g. Valencia, Lisbon, Berlin) running meetups and workshops; includes how to apply to lead an official chapter and what Chapter Leads receive.
- [Blog](https://genaicommunity.eu/blog): Landing for the GenAI Community blog — expert posts on building with generative AI in production (agents, evals, infra), published on Beehiiv (https://genaicommunity.beehiiv.com/). Community experts can apply to write via the form linked on the page.
- [Sponsor](https://genaicommunity.eu/sponsor): Partnership and sponsorship options — host a chapter in your city, or sponsor events such as GenAI Summit EU and GenAI Workshop Fest.
- [Contact](https://genaicommunity.eu/contact): General contact form for questions about chapters, speaking, writing for the blog, press, and more.

## Events

- [GenAI Summit EU](https://genaisummit.eu): The community's annual flagship — two days of technical talks, workshops, and networking in Valencia, Spain.
